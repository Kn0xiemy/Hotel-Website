The History Of Our Hotel

Our story began back in 1984, when our founder George Scotfield opened up his hotel with the idea of setting a standard to what is expected of luxury, comfort and most of all, culture of England. Ever since then, HBT has expanded rapidly, attracting not only locals, but foreign visitors as well.
